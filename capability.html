About Us

Maiwashe Recoveries is a specialist investigative and recovery firm, forming part of the Maiwashe Group. We provide banks, liquidators, government departments, municipalities, state‑owned entities and legal practitioners with actionable intelligence and investigative solutions to uncover, trace and recover concealed or misappropriated assets.

Our approach is professional, discreet and results‑driven, ensuring that investigations support recovery, litigation and insolvency processes while adhering to all legal and regulatory requirements.
